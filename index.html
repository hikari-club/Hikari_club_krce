About K.Ramakrishnan College of Engineering

工学技術と文化の融合

K.Ramakrishnan College of Engineering stands as a beacon of excellence in technical education, where innovation meets cultural diversity. Through our dedicated programs and initiatives, we create an environment that nurtures both technical expertise and global understanding.

HIKARI Club

光 クラブ

Welcome to HIKARI Club - where the brilliance of Japanese culture illuminates our campus. Our name "HIKARI" (光) means "light" in Japanese, symbolizing our mission to brighten the cultural landscape through the celebration of Japanese arts, traditions, and modern pop culture.

Through workshops, cultural exchanges, and events like Japan Talent Fest, we create a vibrant platform for students to explore and embrace Japanese culture while fostering international understanding.

Our Vision

私たちのビジョン

We believe in creating an environment where engineering excellence meets cultural understanding. Our collaboration with Japanese institutions opens doors to unique opportunities, allowing students to engage in enriching experiences that foster a deep appreciation for Japanese culture.

Through cultural exchange programs and immersive workshops, we empower our students to become global citizens ready for success in an increasingly interconnected world.
